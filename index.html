Image to Ascii

Funleash a mesmerizing transformation as your images metamorphose into stunning ASCII art, a captivating fusion of digital aesthetics and retro vibes











Details

This project allows you to convert images into ASCII art. The canvas on the left displays the converted ASCII art, and you can customize the characters used for conversion. To get started, upload an image using the "Load Image" button, customize the characters in the input field, and save the result with the "Save" button. Explore the creative possibilities of ASCII art with this simple yet powerful tool!

The ASCII art is generated using a combination of characters based on the brightness of each pixel in the uploaded image. Adjust the character set in the input field to experiment with different visual effects in your ASCII art creations. Have fun and unleash your creativity!
